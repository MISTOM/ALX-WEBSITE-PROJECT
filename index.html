    ALX JOURNEY

    Thomas Kigarde

    What is ALX ?

    ALX Africa is a leadership, employment, and entrepreneurship development organization. They select, develop, and connect Africa's brightest and most driven young talent. ALX helps us to source and seize meaningful work opportunities. It is the newest addition to the globally recognized African Leadership Group (ALG).


    I stumbled upon ALX through an ad on a social media page. This ad was directed to me probably because I was looking for Software Engineering Jobs online, and it happend that ALX was offering applications for young African Leaders so I seized the chance and clicked on the ad.

    I read their mission to change Africa by bringing up the next Generation Leaders. On a mission to capture one of the 21st century's greatest opportunities.

    Never doubt that a group, a small group of thoughtfull leaders,can change Africa; Indeed it's the only thing that ever will.

    Founder and CEO of Africa Leadership Group Fred Swaniker

    So I took the step of faith and applied for the Software Engineering Program. During the first few days before I started the program, ALX was providing with learning materials, links to usefull recourses i.e -freeCodeCamp- and content to give me the headstart and mindset to Bulldoze through the Application. This was quite usefull and thoughtfull of ALX motivating and lighting the spark.

    A few days later I was accepted to start the Program, now at level One. The key in Level 1 was putting in the time and effort to go out and find the right answers. This builds the Googling skills one needs as a Software Developer. I’ll need more of that to succeed going forward.
    I passed Level one eager to find out what was in store for me on level two. In turn i got a nice looking Selection Part 1 Certificate outlining my missions statement and values.

    Currently I am at level 2 and really excited because I get to get my hands dirty by configuring the server given by installing a webserver,terminal editors E.T.C. The main project was Developing a website and hosting it on the provided server, Yet to find out how this will turn out! Hate to cut off from here but hey, I will live you with a food for thought. . .

    A user interface is like a joke. If you have to explain it, it’s not that good.TOM
    Walking on water and developing software from a specification are easy if both are frozen.
